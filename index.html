About Updato

Updato News is a modern news aggregator that delivers the latest headlines from trusted Indian publishers across seven languages — English, Hindi, Tamil, Bengali, Kannada, Telugu, and Malayalam. Our content is refreshed continuously throughout the day.

Updato is an aggregator: every story shows its original publisher and links directly back to the source article. All article content, headlines, and images remain the property of their respective publishers and authors.
